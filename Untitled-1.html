About Us

Welcome to Shri Agro Tech Farming and Research Center.

We are dedicated to research and innovation in the field of agriculture, striving to improve farming practices and promote sustainable agriculture.

Our team of experts is passionate about agriculture and works tirelessly to develop solutions that address the challenges faced by modern farmers.

From improving crop yields to enhancing soil health, we are at the forefront of agricultural research and development.

Established in 2000, our center has grown to become a leading institution in agricultural research, known for our commitment to excellence and innovation.


Services

  • Research and Development

    • Our R&D team works on innovative projects to develop new farming techniques and technologies that increase productivity and sustainability.

  • Consultancy Services

    • We offer expert consultancy services to help farmers implement best practices and optimize their agricultural operations.

  • Training Programs

    • Our training programs are designed to educate farmers and agribusiness professionals on the latest advancements in agriculture.

  • Farming Solutions
